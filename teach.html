Our Confession of Faith

Rooted in Scripture.
Shaped by History.

Welcome to Newspring Church Kenya. We are affiliated to the Baptist Convention of Kenya and hold to the Baptist statement of faith. Our teachings are drawn from the absolute truth of Scripture, faithfully summarized in documents like the 1689 London Baptist Confession of Faith, and aligned with the Southern Baptist Convention's Baptist Faith and Message. We hold that sound, biblical doctrine is the foundation for true worship, personal holiness, and urgent global mission.

Practical Implications

What This Means for You

This confession is not just a historical document — it is the living heartbeat of our teachings as a Newspring church family. It shapes everything we do.

Our Preaching

Every sermon is rooted in the truth of Scripture — expository, Christ-centered, and applied to our lives today.

Our Worship

We sing, pray, and gather in response to who God is and what He has done — worship that is truthful and heartfelt.

Our Community

We love, serve, and encourage one another based on these shared truths — a family accountable to God's Word.

Our Mission

We reach out with compassion and conviction, calling all to repentance and faith in Christ — locally and globally.
